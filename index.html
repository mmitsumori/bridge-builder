How to play

Use Tab to move between planks, then press Enter or Space to place a plank on the bridge. Each plank's number is added to your running total. Reach the target number exactly to complete the bridge and send your character across!

If a plank would push you over the target it will bounce back — pick a smaller one. Press R to reset the bridge or N for a new game at any time.
